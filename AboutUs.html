About Us

The Young Progressives Party (YPP) was formed in 2017, in line with the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria 1999 (as amended), founded by people of like-minds upon social democratic principles and inclusive governance, to provide a veritable platform for all Nigerians irrespective of tribe and religion.

We Believe:
  • In God
  • That leadership must be by example and without greed
  • That pride in our country must be restored
  • That the security of our citizen must be prioritzed
  • That the common vision of our people must be achieved
  • That the empowerment and upliftment of our women must be hastned
  • That the energy of our youth must be galvanized and fully deployed
  • That the wealth of experience of our elders must be tapped
  • That the knowledge and skills of professionals must be utilized
  • That the voice of local community must be heard
  • That the diplomacy and international cooperation must be improved and sustained
